About Us

The Bulletin team

Sunnyside Bulletin Board is a non-profit community initiative dedicated to helping neighbors in Sunnyside publish and discover information about garage sale items. Whether you're decluttering, looking for something unique, or simply want to support local reuse, our platform is here for you.

Our Purpose

Our mission is to make reusing and rehoming easy, accessible, and community-driven. Through a virtual bulletin board and online tools, we empower residents to connect, post listings, and find treasures just around the corner. We do this work for the love of the community and we would never ask you for any payment.

Who we serve

For people who are looking for information about rental spaces, cars or handyman and fr people renting out units or selling their cars or offering services to meet directly and communicate efficiently. We are here for the resident and the visitor/passerby, we serve all with a heart of gold.

Our Community

Sunnyside is a vibrant and diverse community known for its strong sense of togetherness and local pride. Nestled in a sunny corner of the city, Sunnyside is home to families, students, retirees, and small business owners who contribute to a welcoming and supportive atmosphere. The area boasts tree-lined streets, local markets, and parks that serve as gathering spaces for events and everyday relaxation.

Residents of Sunnyside are actively involved in neighborhood initiatives, such as clean-up drives, youth programs, and cultural festivals, which celebrate the community's rich blend of backgrounds and traditions. With its warm spirit and shared commitment to growth and inclusion, Sunnyside continues to be a place where people feel connected and inspired to make a positive impact.
