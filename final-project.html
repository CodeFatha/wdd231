Bulletin Board

Final Project Wireframe

Project name:

SunnySide Bulletin Board

Reason: This specifies the purpose and the catchment area served by the site so new visitors know what to expect and the area location. This will reduce irrelevent posts

Optional domain availability

bulletin.info - this would be prefixed with the area by way of a subdomain eg london.bulletin.info

Color Schema

  • Header and footer background - #053f72
  • Header and footer text - #fff
  • Link hover - #ffff00
  • Menu background - #641313

Typography

  • Menu and footer - Sour Gummy
  • Body - Roboto
  • Banner text - Nunito

Site purpose

For posting and viewing advertisements for houses and rooms to rent, cars on sale and handyman services

Scenarios

For people who are looking for information about rental spaces, cars or handyman and fr people renting out units or selling their cars or offering services to meet directly and communicate efficiently
